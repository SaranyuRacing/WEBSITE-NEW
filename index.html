Saranyu Racing

"Welcome to our community of motorsport-driven students! We are a dedicated team of young engineers, innovators, and racing enthusiasts who share a common passion for speed, technology, and performance. Our journey is fueled by a deep interest in motorsports, pushing the boundaries of engineering and design to create high-performance vehicle."

Our Mission:

Innovation & Excellence

To push the boundaries of electric vehicle technology by designing and developing high-performance, energy-efficient, and cutting-edge race cars. We strive to integrate advanced engineering principles, creative problem-solving, and innovative technologies to stay ahead in the evolving world of electric mobility.

Enhancing Performance through Engineering Excellence

To integrate advanced simulations, data-driven optimizations, and precision manufacturing in our vehicle design to achieve superior performance on the racetrack.

Developing Next-Generation Motorsport Engineers

To equip students with hands-on experience in motorsport engineering, vehicle dynamics, telemetry, and race strategy, preparing them for careers in the automotive and motorsports industries.

Advancing Electric Motorsports

To develop high-performance electric race cars that compete at the highest levels of student motorsports, pushing the boundaries of speed, efficiency, and sustainability.
