We're

Saranyu Racing

"We are students from Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology and proud members of the Dr. AIT Motorsports Club. Under this club, Team Saranyu Racing is a passionate group dedicated to design and build FSAE cars. Our team brings together students from various engineering disciplines to create FSAE cars, that compete in international competitions."

Visit our official college website

Team Division:

Structural

The Structural Department designs a lightweight yet strong chassis that ensures safety and performance. It emphasizes material optimization, structural integrity, and ergonomic cockpit design.

Vehicle Dynamics

Vehicle Dynamics focuses on optimizing suspension, steering, braking, and wheels to enhance handling, stability, and control. It ensures precise maneuverability and balanced performance using engineering and motorsport principles.

Powertrain

The Powertrain system transfers engine power to the wheels, focusing on efficiency and performance tuning. Key tasks include optimizing intake and gear ratios for ideal acceleration and top speed on the track.

Electrical

The Electrical Team handles all wiring, sensors, and power distribution to ensure safe and efficient car operation. They also implement safety systems and support data acquisition across departments.

Non Technical

The non-technical division manages essential operations through teams like marketing, business development, and procurement. They handle branding, partnerships, sourcing, social media engagement, and financial oversight. Additionally, documentation ensures organized records and transparency.
